# LVB Acquisition (lvb-acquisition)
LVB Acquisition was the parent company of Biomet, a medical device company specializing in orthopedic medical devices, before merging with Zimmer Holdings to form Zimmer Biomet. The combined entity operates as Zimmer Biomet Holdings and no public APIs are published under the LVB Acquisition brand.
